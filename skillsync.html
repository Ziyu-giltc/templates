Built by teams, for teams

SkillSync started as an internal tool to solve our own coordination headaches. We were tired of context-switching between five different apps just to understand project status.

After iterating based on real team feedback, we expanded into a full product. Today, SkillSync helps over 10,000 teams across 38 countries work more efficiently without the complexity of enterprise tools.

Our mission: give teams a workspace that's powerful enough to be useful but simple enough to adopt on day one.
